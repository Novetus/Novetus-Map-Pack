Killbots tend to enjoy their artificial lives in the scorching heat of the Chaos Canyon. What once was home to ancient ROBLOXian gladiators, is now nothing but Killbot property.

Settings:
Each wave spawns 30 enemies maximum.
2 to 4 more enemies spawn per new wave.
Boss battles happen every 5 waves.
Heavies start spawning after wave 1.
Agilities start spawning after wave 3.
Commanders start spawning after wave 5.
Commanders have a 1 in 3 chance to spawn after wave 5.
